## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please do **NOT** open an issue.

Instead, please email security@package.broker with the details. We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours and work with you to resolve the issue.

We appreciate your responsible disclosure.
